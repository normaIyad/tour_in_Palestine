Cycling is becoming increasingly popular in Palestine. You can rent a bike and explore the countryside, taking in the scenic beauty and experiencing the local culture at your own pace.

Hike in the West Bank

The West Bank offers fantastic hiking opportunities. Consider trekking in the Wadi Qelt, or along sections of the Abraham Path. These trails offer breathtaking landscapes, ancient ruins, and a chance to connect with nature.
